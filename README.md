Sarkari Sahayak

Transforming Welfare Access into Welfare Intelligence

Sarkari Sahayak is an AI-powered welfare intelligence platform designed to simplify access to government schemes and transform welfare delivery across India. The platform enables citizens to discover schemes, assess eligibility, receive multilingual AI assistance with voice support, generate application guidance, and plan family welfare journeys. Beyond citizen services, it provides policymakers and CSC operators with policy intelligence, outcome prediction, digital twin simulations, intervention planning, offline assistance capabilities, early warning systems, impact monitoring, and a National Welfare Intelligence Observatory.

---

Project Overview

Sarkari Sahayak bridges the gap between citizens and government welfare schemes by offering a unified, multilingual, and AI-powered platform. The system integrates citizen-centric services with policy intelligence capabilities to improve welfare accessibility, transparency, and governance.

---

Problem Statement

"An AI assistant that helps people understand and apply for government schemes in their local language."

Millions of citizens remain unaware of government welfare schemes or struggle to understand eligibility requirements, documentation needs, and application procedures. Sarkari Sahayak addresses this challenge through an inclusive and intelligent welfare ecosystem.

Key Features

1. AI-Powered Scheme Discovery

Natural language chat grounded in verified government sources using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

2. Smart Eligibility Matching

Personalized scheme recommendations based on age, income, location, occupation, and family structure.

3. Welfare Navigator

Transforms life goals into actionable welfare roadmaps with scheme sequences and timelines.

4. Family Welfare Planning

Coordinates benefits across all household members to maximize welfare coverage.

5. 5-Year Welfare Roadmap

Projects welfare journeys using life-event simulations and pre-identifies future scheme eligibility.

6. Explainable AI Decisions

Provides transparent reasoning behind recommendations and eligibility outcomes.

7. Voice-First Interface

Speech recognition and text-to-speech support in multiple Indian languages.

8. Offline Assistance

Core services remain accessible in low-connectivity environments with synchronization support.

9. CSC Agent Dashboard

Empowers Common Service Center operators to assist citizens efficiently.

10. Analytics & Research Intelligence

Supports policymakers and NGOs through welfare insights and regional gap analysis.
